## 0. The commitment, and the question it forces

Until #204, Φ[ρ] was an internal modeling device of the soliton
sandbox. #204 made it **load-bearing**: the no-signaling verdict — the
only nonlinear channel is the retarded gravitational field — is a
statement *about* Φ[ρ]. A Schrödinger–Newton-type self-coupling is
therefore a commitment of the theory, not a convenience of the sandbox.

That is not a defect; it is a gift. SN-type self-gravity is exactly
what macromolecule interferometry, levitated optomechanics, and the
gravitationally-induced-entanglement programs are built to test — the
**nearest-term experimental falsification channel BAM has**, far closer
than the standing neutrino-sector cards. It belongs on the register,
and this PR puts it there.

But the lab predictions hinge on a subtlety #198 flagged and never
resolved ("*a throat's own self-field is not a pilot wave for itself in
superposition*"):

> Does Φ source from ρ of the **universal wave** — so that both
> interferometer branches gravitate at ~half weight each, giving the
> full SN phenomenology (branch attraction, interference inhibition,
> wavepacket self-trapping)? Or from the **conditional / actual
> soliton configuration** — the mass riding the lump through one
> branch, so the SN signatures are absent?

The two readings give different lab predictions. #204 forces the
program to pick, in writing. This audit makes the pick the only honest
way available to a classical-field program: **by asking the committed
dynamics** — the adjudication experiments run on the same locked
ψ–Φ–q structure that #198 and #204 audited.

**The answer, up front:** the dynamics picks *conditional*. The bound
throat-soliton crosses a beamsplitter whole; the mass-carrying field
never co-occupies interferometer arms in the lab regime. BAM therefore
predicts **null** at the SN scale (against Schrödinger–Newton) and
**null** in the gravitational-entanglement channel (against quantized
gravity) — two discriminating near-term predictions — while existing
data exclude nothing (margins computed below).

## 1. Why the question is decidable at all

At the **fundamental** level there is no ambiguity: the committed
Poisson/wave equation sources from the 3-space field density
`ψ² + μq²`, full stop — that is the equation #180 locked, #198 used,
and #204 made load-bearing. The ambiguity lives entirely in the
**effective CM description** (whose emergence from the 3-space field is
the standing open item), where the Born measure |ψ_CM|² and the
gravitational source can *part ways*: the measure keeps its statistical
role (#198) whatever the source does. The question therefore reduces to
a dynamical one the sandbox can answer:

> When a bound throat-soliton meets a beamsplitter in the lab regime
> (kinetic ≪ binding), does the mass-carrying field co-occupy the two
> branches?

## 2. E1 — the beamsplitter: the dynamics makes the pick

The relaxed 1D throat-soliton of the committed structure (M = 4,
chemical potential μ_c = −10.5, ordered core q_max = 0.88) launched at
a barrier over a velocity sweep, against a linear control (the same
profile with self-binding switched off):

| v | BAM (R, T) | linear control (R, T) |
|---:|---|---|
| 0.3 | 0.9999, 0.0001 | — |
| 0.4 | 1.0000, 0.0000 | 0.82, 0.13 |
| 0.5 | 0.91, 0.09 | — |
| 0.6 | 0.0002, 0.9998 | 0.69, 0.20 |
| 0.7 | 0.0000, 1.0000 | — |
| 0.8 | 0.0000, 1.0000 | 0.47, 0.36 |
| 1.0 | 0.0000, 0.9998 | 0.29, 0.52 |
| 1.2 | 0.0000, 0.9992 | 0.18, 0.64 |

- **Whole-body transport:** max(R, T) ≥ 0.95 at 7 of 8 velocities;
  branch co-occupation is confined to a single grid point (the
  threshold window, bracket (0.5, 0.6)); deep below threshold the
  leakage into the untaken branch is ~10⁻⁴.
- **The linear control co-occupies both branches across the entire
  sweep** (max(R,T) never exceeds 0.82, falls to 0.47) — the
  whole-body behavior is the *self-binding*, not the barrier.
- **The regime mapping:** even the sandbox threshold sits at
  kinetic/binding ≈ 0.01. A 300 m/s molecular beam sits at
  (v/c)²/2 ≈ 5×10⁻¹³ against structural binding at the rest-mass scale
  — eleven orders below the sandbox threshold, which itself leaks only
  10⁻⁴. Splitting the *mass* between branches is the relativistic/QFT
  regime (the #200 pair-creation domain), not the interferometry
  regime.

**The pick, made by the equations:** in the effective CM description, Φ
sources from the **conditional / actual configuration**. #198's aside
is now a measured statement. (The universal-marginal dressing of #204's
entangled-sector test remains what it was: the deliberately *maximal*
channel for the signaling audit — if even that is causal when retarded,
the weaker conditional sourcing is a fortiori.)

## 3. E2 — the branch-attraction channel: real, exactly Newtonian, weight f²

If mass *did* co-occupy branches, the committed dynamics would make
them fall together. Measured: a hand-prepared 50/50 branch split under
the committed dynamics contracts at exactly the field-equation
Newtonian rate (measured/predicted separation = 1.000 → 1.005 from t=1
to t=5, against the exact periodic-pair prediction
d̈ = −2πGM(1 − 2d/L), no fit) until the branches merge.

So the SN signatures are governed by one number: the co-occupied mass
fraction f per branch. Attraction rate f²·Gm/d², SN phase
f²·Gm²t/(ℏd), inhibition threshold rescaled accordingly. Pure SN is
f = 1/2-per-branch; **BAM's measured f is exponentially small in
binding/kinetic** — already 10⁻⁴ at kinetic/binding ~ 10⁻², with the
lab at ~10⁻¹². The committed prediction: **the SN signatures are
absent** — not because the channel is absent (it is measured, above),
but because the weight is zero.

## 4. E3 — the classical channel cannot entangle (the BMV discriminator)

A product two-throat state evolved under the BAM mean-field gravity —
Φ sourced by ρ₁+ρ₂, V = Φ(x₁) + Φ(x₂), a classical common potential —
stays exactly unentangled: **entanglement entropy 0 at machine
precision** over the full run. Structurally so: a c-number field enters
as a sum of local potentials, and time-dependent local Hamiltonians
have zero entangling power (the conditional-sourcing variant is a
stochastic local channel — no local channel creates entanglement). The
**quantized-gravity comparator** — the pairwise operator
V(x₁,x₂) = 2πG|x₁−x₂| at the same coupling — entangles the same state
to S = 0.148 by t = 4.

BAM's gravity is classical in *either* sourcing reading, so BAM
predicts **strictly null** in Bose–Marletto–Vedral-type gravitational
entanglement witnesses, where quantized gravity predicts an O(1) phase
(§5). A classical-gravity program cannot survive an observed
gravitational entanglement witness — the sharpest falsifier the
commitment buys.

  ω_SN(Si) ≈ **0.05 s⁻¹** (Δx_zp = 4.9 pm input) — beyond any current

## 6. What is and is not established (honest scope)

**Established:**
- Φ[ρ] is a commitment (#204), and the committed dynamics itself
  adjudicates the #198 sourcing ambiguity — whole-body beamsplitter
  transport, measured, with the linear control as the contrast;
- the branch-attraction channel is real and exactly the field-equation
  rate (0.1–0.5%); the lab weight f² is exponentially zero;
- the classical channel cannot entangle (machine zero, vs S = 0.15 for
  the quantized comparator at the same coupling);
- existing data exclude nothing (mass margin ~10⁵; phase margin ~10¹⁷).

**Not established (the conditions):**
1. The adjudication is a 1D structural/regime statement — the
   *mechanism* (exponential smallness of branch co-occupation in
   binding/kinetic) — not a lab-precision number; the 3D repeat is a
   follow-up.
2. **How** the empty branch guides without gravitating — the emergence
   of the CM pilot wave from the 3-space field — remains the standing
   open item (#198 condition 2): narrowed again (its gravitational
   sourcing is now fixed), not closed.
3. The lab table uses stated O(1) conventions (m*, ω_SN inputs); the
   discrimination matrix is convention-robust.
4. A BMV null alone would not single out BAM (all classical-gravity
   theories share it); the committed signature is the *combination* —
   SN-null + BMV-null + fringes intact at every mass.
5. If the throat sector ever requires f > 0 (a derived CM-level
   wave-mass fraction), the SN bounds of this audit propagate back as
   constraints — the outcome-(2) channel, kept open.
