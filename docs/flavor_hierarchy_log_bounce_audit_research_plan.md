# Flavor hierarchy audit from logarithmic throat bounce lengths (PR #134)

PRs #88 and #132 established that the throat bounce action is **logarithmic** in
the healing length, `S = c·L*(ε)` with `L*(ε) = (rs/2) ln(1/ε)`, and that masses
generated by tunnelling are `m ~ e^{−S}`. This PR audits the question that
raises: **can the flavor hierarchy — the three-generation mass spread — be read
off these logarithmic throat bounce lengths?** The honest finding: the
log-bounce gives a **power-law** mass `m ∝ ε^p`, which governs the chargeless
**neutrino** sector (the only genuine tunnelling sector) in form and ordering
but overshoots its value; the charged-lepton (winding) and quark (cavity)
hierarchies are **different mechanisms**. The flavor hierarchy is **not unified**
by the log-bounce — but the log-bounce explains *why* the neutrino values are
residual: the steep power makes masses hypersensitive to the throat parameters.

## The mechanism: a logarithmic bounce gives a power-law mass

If `S = c·L*(ε) = c·(rs/2) ln(1/ε)`, then a tunnelling mass is

```
m = m_0 e^{−S} = m_0 e^{−c(rs/2) ln(1/ε)} = m_0 ε^{c·rs/2} = m_0 ε^p,   p = c·rs/2.
```

So the logarithm in the bounce length turns the exponential `e^{−S}` into a
**pure power law** in the throat penetration depth `ε`. Masses are powers of
`ε`, not exponentials of a linear quantity — the defining signature of the
log-bounce mechanism (identity `e^{−cL*} = ε^p` verified).

## The neutrino sector IS the log-bounce sector (form & ordering, not value)

The neutrino is the only genuine tunnelling sector: `k = 0`, chargeless, the
neck not EM-propped (#86/#88), so `m_ν = m_D e^{−S} ∝ ε^p` with `p ≈ 4.8`
(#112). The generation healing lengths `ε_n ∝ 1/χ_n` (#79/#113) give the right
**ordering** (`χ_n` decreasing ⟹ `ε_n` increasing ⟹ less suppression ⟹ heavier
⟹ normal ordering), but the steep power **amplifies** the modest `χ_n` spread:

| | gen 1 | gen 2 | gen 3 |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| `χ_n` (#79) | 0.304 | 0.097 | 0.039 |
| `ε_n ∝ 1/χ_n` ratio | 1.0 | 3.13 | 7.79 |

A `~×2` spread in `ε` becomes `2^4.8 ≈ 28×` in mass — exactly the **×28
overshoot** of #113. So the log-bounce governs the neutrino hierarchy's form and
ordering, with the value residual.

## Charged leptons and quarks are NOT log-bounce

  - **Charged leptons** are Dirac (`c₁ = ±1`, the neck EM-propped, no
    tunnelling, #86/#88); their masses come from the **winding** ladder `β·k²`
    (#71), not the bounce. Their observed `ln(m)` is irregular (gen-differences
    `5.33, 2.82` — ratio `0.53`), not a clean power law.
  - **Quarks** are shell-resolving **cavity** overtones (#77–#80, the `n_part`
    sector), not throat-tunnelling; their `ln(m)` is irregular too (up-type
    differences `6.37, 4.91` — ratio `0.77`).

So the flavor hierarchy is a **three-mechanism structure** — bounce (ν),
winding (charged leptons), cavity (quarks) — not a single log-bounce phenomenon.

## Why the flavor values are residual: log-bounce hypersensitivity

The power law `m ∝ ε^p` has `∂ln m/∂ln ε = p`. With `p` a few (`4.8` for ν),
masses are **hypersensitive** to the throat penetration depth:

| `p` | `∂ln m/∂ln ε` | mass spread for `×2` ε |
|---:|---:|---:|
| 1.0 | 1.0 | 2.0× |
| 4.8 | 4.8 | 27.9× |

So the irreducibility of the flavor values (#108) is a **consequence** of the
exponential mass-action relation — the log-bounce amplification — not a separate
mystery: even a fully-determined geometry would leave the precise masses
hypersensitive to the `ε` residual (#112).
