## Verdict

**QUARK_HIERARCHY_IS_FLAVOR_PUZZLE_NOT_RG_RUNNING.** THE QUARK HIERARCHY IS THE FLAVOR PUZZLE, NOT α_s RUNNING — REFINING #97. PR #97 diagnosed the quark inter-generation hierarchy as dynamical (QCD-RG) and the program's one non-geometric sector. This probe takes the first step on that "right route" and, in doing so, tests and sharpens the mechanism.

QUARK MASS RATIOS ARE RG-INVARIANT. QCD's mass anomalous dimension γ_m(α_s) is flavor-universal — the same function for every quark — so under renormalisation-group running every mass is multiplied by the SAME factor, which cancels in any ratio: m_i(μ₂)/m_j(μ₂) = m_i(μ₁)/m_j(μ₁). Quark mass RATIOS are therefore RG-invariant; α_s running sets the overall scale, not the hierarchy. So the irregular hierarchy is NOT generated by QCD running — PR #97's "QCD-RG" attribution was too specific.

IT IS THE FLAVOR PUZZLE. If the hierarchy is not a running effect, it is set by the Yukawa couplings at the fundamental scale — free inputs in the Standard Model, the flavor puzzle. The irregularity PR #97 measured (m_c/m_u ≈ 588 vs m_t/m_c ≈ 136; up/down asymmetric) is exactly its signature: the Yukawas are hierarchical and irregular for reasons no current theory — geometric or otherwise — derives. This is a universal open problem, not a BAM-specific failing.

THE QUARK YUKAWAS OVERFLOW THE SHELL. The quark shell overtones (k=0, n=3,4,5; PR #77/#83) have ω = 3.82, 4.76, 5.70 — a mass range of only ×1.49 — whereas the up-type quarks span m_t/m_u ≈ 8×10⁴. The geometric shell capacity is overflowed by ~×5×10⁴; n_part is the compensator for that overflow. By contrast the charged-lepton hierarchy (also a flavor puzzle, m_τ/m_e ≈ 3477) is fit to sub-percent by the BAM winding ladder (k ∈ {1,3,5}, PR #71): the winding quantum number plus the closure machinery has the dynamic range that the compressed quark shell overtones lack — which is why BAM captures the lepton flavor structure geometrically but the quark hierarchy overflows.

GEOMETRIC STRUCTURE vs FLAVOR-PUZZLE MAGNITUDES. The quark sector splits cleanly: BAM captures the STRUCTURE — six quarks (3×2, PR #69), the Z₂ up/down partition, the k=0 shell channel, three generations from k_5 (PR #72) — counting and quantum numbers, robust; the Yukawa MAGNITUDES (the irregular hierarchy) are the flavor puzzle — RG-invariant (not running), overflowing the shell (not the overtones), with n_part as the compensator.

HONEST SCOPE. This REFINES PR #97 without overturning it: the core conclusion (the quark hierarchy is dynamical / non-geometric, the program's one such sector, n_part can only compensate it) stands; the mechanism is sharpened from "QCD-RG running" to "the flavor puzzle (irregular Yukawas)", because quark mass ratios are RG-invariant.
